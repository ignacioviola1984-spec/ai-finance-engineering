# Lumen Inc. Travel & Expense Policy

Purpose: define approval and reimbursement rules for employee expenses.
Effective date: 2026-02-01.

Approval: expenses up to USD 1,000 require manager approval. Expenses from
USD 1,000 to USD 5,000 require director approval. Above USD 5,000 require VP
of Finance approval.
Travel: economy class for flights under 6 hours; business class permitted for
flights over 6 hours with director approval. Hotel cap of USD 250 per night.
Reimbursement: submit within 30 days of the expense. Reimbursements are paid in
the next payroll cycle after approval.
Non-reimbursable: personal entertainment, fines, and first-class upgrades.
