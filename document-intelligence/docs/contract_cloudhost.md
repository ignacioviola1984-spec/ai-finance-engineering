# Master Services Agreement - CloudHost Infrastructure Inc.

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Lumen Inc.
("Customer") and CloudHost Infrastructure Inc. ("Provider").

Effective date: 2025-09-01.
Initial term: 24 months. The Agreement automatically renews for successive
12-month terms unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at
least 60 days before the end of the then-current term.

Fees: USD 18,500 per month for compute and storage, billed monthly in advance.
Annual price increases are capped at 5%.
Payment terms: net 30 days from invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at
1.5% per month.

Termination: either party may terminate for material breach with 30 days written
notice if the breach is not cured. Customer may terminate for convenience with
90 days notice, subject to payment for the remaining current term.
Governing law: State of Delaware, USA.
Data: Provider maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance and 99.9% uptime SLA.
